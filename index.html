GPA Calculator

The GPA Calculator enables students to easily calculate their GPA scores and track their academic progress per semester.

Key Features

Easy GPA Calculation

Calculate your GPA scores quickly and easily by entering your subject marks and obtained grades. Using the table to help you convert and understand how it works.

Track Academic Progress

Keep track of your academic performance over multiple semesters and see how you improve over time.

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy a clean, intuitive interface designed to make GPA calculation straightforward and hassle-free.

About This Project

This project was inspired by the many students I've encountered who struggled to calculate their GPA scores when enrolling in university or tracking their semester performance. The GPA Calculator aims to simplify this process, helping students stay on top of their academic achievements.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
